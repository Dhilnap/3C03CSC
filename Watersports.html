Flyboarding futuristic ride has a board which is propelled into the air, enabling you to hydro-fly! Prepare to indulge in this unforgettable experience as you soar above the sea and watch the world with a top view. April to October Lhaviyani Atoll, Maafushi Island
Jet Skiing faster variation of water skiing, as you and your partner sit on a jet ski and drive away into the blue sea. Channel some major 007 vibes as you zip through the waves! April to October All resort islands, Malé. Contact agencies to ship you to beaches where you can jetski.
Fun tube Group ride consists of a bed-like tube where you can sit with your squad, and zoom through the bright blue ocean! This is the epitome of laid-back fun combined with the thrill of speed. Do not miss out on this crazy watersports in Maldives. April to October Can avail this in almost every resort beach in the country.They are popular in Malé, Baa Atoll, Ari Atolls and Kaafu Atoll.
Kayaking One of the most popular adventure sports in the Maldives, and for a good reason too. Explore and experience the coastline at your own pace as you become part of the swaying waves, and watch your stresses melt away! April to October Even small beaches in local islands have kayaking facilities so that you can rent a kayak on almost every beach in Maldives
Kitesurfing Twice as thrilling as surfing as you have the added pull of the tropical winds! Experience the ocean like never before as you become one with the waves, and soar into the wake with your mighty kite. This can be a great gateway sport into surfing as well. April to October All resort islands, Malé and Hulumalé
Parasailing Fly as high as a kite, soaring over the pristine blue waters of the Indian Ocean. Kitesurfing is an exciting watersport in the Maldives to try out, as it offers a stunning bird's eye view of the archipelago as you're tethered to a speedboat! April to October All resort islands, Malé, Hulumalé
Surfing No beach holiday is complete without a surfing trip! Take the waves by storm as you ride the ocean in all its glory in Maldives. You can try stand-up paddling and then proceed to surf as well. April to October The best wakes are in Baa Atoll and Thulusdhoo Islands, but you can enjoy surfing at any beach with an instructor.
